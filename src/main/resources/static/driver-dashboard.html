Driver Tips

Before you start

  • Keep your vehicle clean and check fuel, tyres, and lights.
  • Start the ride a few minutes early to avoid last‑minute rush.
  • Verify passenger name and pickup point before moving.

On the road

  • Drive smoothly and avoid sudden braking or speeding.
  • Share ETA updates via chat if you are running late.
  • End the ride in the app only after all passengers exit safely.
